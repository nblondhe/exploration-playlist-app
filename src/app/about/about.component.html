Exploration allows users to find new music by building intentional, smooth-flowing playlists based off of music they already love.

One of my favorite features of Spotify is the delivery of recommendations based on listening habits over a time.

I wanted to access the same awesome recommendation engine, but with greater choice and granularity. Exploration gathers a user's most recent saved tracks and provides recommendations, showing exactly which user-saved track they are derived from.

I hope Exploration leads you to excellent music finds!
