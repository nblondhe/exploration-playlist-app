Exploration allows users to find new music by building intentional, flowing playlists based off of music they already love.

One of my favorite features of Spotify is the delivery of recommendations based on listening habits over a time. By providing recommendations based off of user-saved tracks, Exploration exposes the same awesome recommendation engine with greater choice and granularity for the user.

I hope Exploration leads you to excellent music finds!
